## Overview

v0.6.0 adds advanced visual effects and compositing features.

### Key Features

1. **Advanced Effects**
   - Drop shadows (proper)
      - Inner shadows
      - Gradients as effects
   - Blur effects on layers
   - Glow effects

2. **Compositing**
   - Clipping masks
   - Alpha masks
   - Advanced blend modes
   - Group layers

3. **Color Adjustments**
   - Color curves
   - Levels adjustment
   - Channel mixer
   - Color balance
